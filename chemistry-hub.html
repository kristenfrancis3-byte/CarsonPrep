Suggested Path

Where Carson Should Start

To prevent overwhelm and build confidence, Carson should work through Chemistry in this order:

  • 1 → Atomic Structure & Periodic Trends
  • 2 → Bonding, Geometry & IMFs
  • 3 → Stoichiometry & Reactions
  • 4 → Gas Laws and states of matter
  • 5 → Thermochemistry basics
  • Then layer in Solutions, Acids/Bases, Equilibrium, Kinetics as time allows.
