Quick Start for Carson

There is no strict daily schedule here on purpose. This portal is organized by subjects and study themes so you can follow your focus and still work on what matters most for KAAC.

  1. Start in Chemistry → Foundations. Chemistry is about 40% of KAAC Science.
  2. Layer in Buzzer Training. Use the strategy and buzzing pages to train your early-buzz instincts.
  3. Explore Physics, Earth & Space, Biology, and A/H. Use hubs and topic pages when you’re ready.
  4. Come back to topics. There’s no “falling behind” – just making progress.
