5. Periodic Trends

Overview

(Appear CONSTANTLY in KAAC questions.)

  • Atomic radius: down → increases; right → decreases
  • Ionization energy: down → decreases; right → increases (exceptions at Group 13 & 16)
  • Electronegativity: down → decreases; right → increases
  • Electron affinity: down → decreases; right → increases
  • Metallic character: down → increases; right → decreases

Mnemonic: “Down bigger, right smaller; right stronger, up stronger.”

Practice Questions

  1. 1. Atomic radius increases in which direction on the periodic table?

    Down a group and to the left

  2. 2. What periodic trend measures the ability of an atom to attract electrons in a bond?

    Electronegativity

  3. 3. Which increases across a period: ionization energy or metallic character?

    Ionization energy

  4. 4. Which noble gas has the highest electron affinity? Trick question — explain.

    None — noble gases have positive electron affinities.

  5. 5. Which element is more metallic: sodium or aluminum?

    Sodium

Periodic Trends – Buzz-in-First-Three-Words

  • “This trend increases…” → Atomic radius
  • “Ability to attract…” → Electronegativity
  • “Energy required to…” → Ionization energy
  • “This trend decreases…” → Metallic character (across)
  • “More negative electron…” → Electron affinity
